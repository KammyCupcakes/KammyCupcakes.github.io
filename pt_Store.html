This page was made for my HTML5 final back in 2020 during my fall semester at BHCC (My Junior Year). This page was made using only HTML and CSS. When making this page I had to use alot of the things we had learned over the semester and I spent a lot of time working on these pages. I created a fake Pizza Shop website for my final and the first page is the home page giving a brief description of the pizza company "I" am running and where we deliver to; With the contact information at the bottom of the page. My second page is the menu page where I used tables and links to set them up the way I wanted. The menu contains the pizzas and the sides we sell. With that the links under the food pictures takes you to the third and final page and this is just a simple delivery form for the customer fill out and get it delivered to whatever location as long as it is in our boundaries.
